Who are we?
Founded by a biochemist and a microbiologist, HebeKombucha is a privately-held company crafting their probiotics beverages in San Marcos, California. Both founders, two long-time users of probiotics, not only share their passion for probiotics and their dedication to the pursuit of science, but also their awe for the gifts of nature. After many years of doing science together, both set out to found HebeKombucha in 2016, driven by the deep conviction that the maintenance of good health and well-being starts with a quality diet, and that good nutrition should and can become everyone’s most affordable medicine.
Our Mission
We at HebeKombucha believe that healthy nutrition has to be as close to nature as possible (most use the term “fresh”), be as raw as possible (most use the term “unprocessed”), and as untainted by synthetic chemicals as possible, in order to deserve to be called and served as "nutrition." This is the mission we follow every day while we are brewing and composing our uniquely crafted and flavored probiotics beverages for you.

For the good of human health - for the good of the planet.
