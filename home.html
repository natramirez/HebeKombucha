Behind the Hebe
Hebe, whose name originates from a Greek word meaning "prime of life," was the ancient Greek goddess of youth. She was the cup bearer of the gods, so she had the privilege and task of serving ambrosia (a bread-like substance) and nectar (a mythical beverage) to the Olympians to maintain their immortality. Well, we at HebeKombucha do not have the secret recipe for making nectar, and certainly cannot promise immortality, but we do know much about how to craft and serve kombucha and water kefir, two extraordinary probiotic beverages offering a myriad of health benefits.
Brewed Naturally
We brew and craft natural flavor-enriched kombucha and water kefir by blending old tradition with the latest nutritional science. We did not settle with the ancient recipes, but set out to make two already great products of nature even better. We do so by “infusing” our all-natural, raw kombucha and water kefir brews with just the right amount of carefully resourced, all-organic fruits and herbs, rich in health-boosting vitamins, polyphenols and free-radical fighting antioxidants. We brew our creations only in small batches to ensure quality and flavor in our products.
Our Signature Products
We are especially proud of crafting and serving our signature Aronia Kombucha and Black Mission Fig Water Kefir products. We infuse both probiotic beverages with just the right amount of aronia (also known as black chokeberry) and black mission figs, both U.S. grown “super fruits” packed with immune-boosting anthocyanins and other natural antioxidants.
Earth-friendly Ingredients
All of our fruits and herbs are organic, thereby reducing the harmful impact of synthetic pesticides on the environment. And, more than 90% of the fruits going into our probiotics products are resourced from local and California growers. By keeping it local, we are reducing our carbon footprint while supporting local farmers and enriching local communities. Go planet Earth!
